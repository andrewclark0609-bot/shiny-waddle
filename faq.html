Frequently asked questions

Can't find what you need here? Our support page gets you straight to a person.

Ordering & configuration

Can I change the parts in a build?

Within each tier, you can adjust storage, warranty length, and case colour on the configure page. The core CPU/GPU/motherboard combination for each tier is fixed, since those parts are validated together during burn-in — swapping one individually would mean re-testing the whole build. If you want something outside the four tiers, get in touch and we'll see what's possible.

Which build is right for me?

SIGNAL-0.5 and SIGNAL-1 are aimed at 1080p–1440p gaming, SIGNAL-3 at 1440p ultra settings, and SIGNAL-9 at 4K with ray tracing. If you're not sure, tell us what you play and at what resolution via support and we'll point you to the right tier.

Do builds come with Windows installed?

Every unit ships with Windows pre-installed and activated, along with drivers for all installed components, ready to switch on and use.

Are a monitor, keyboard, or mouse included?

No — every build is the PC only. This keeps the price down for people who already have peripherals, and lets you choose your own monitor and input devices to match your setup.

Shipping & delivery

How long does delivery take?

UK orders typically arrive in 5–7 business days from when your build enters the bench. International delivery times vary by region — estimates for Europe, North America, and the rest of the world are shown at checkout before you pay.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes. We ship to most countries worldwide. Shipping cost is calculated by region at checkout — just make sure to select the shipping option matching your actual location.

Will I be charged customs or import fees?

If you're ordering from outside the UK, your country's customs authority may charge import duties or handling fees on arrival. These aren't included in the price you pay us, and the amount is set by your local government — not by SIGNAL.

How is a PC this heavy packaged for shipping?

Units ship in reinforced double-boxing with the case internals braced to prevent movement in transit — the same approach most PC builders use for shipping fully assembled systems.

Burn-in & testing

What actually happens during burn-in?

Every unit runs 72 hours of mixed CPU/GPU stress testing and real game loops before it ships, with clocks, temperatures, and frame-time data logged continuously. Anything that drifts outside spec gets pulled and rebuilt rather than shipped.

Do I actually get a copy of my unit's burn-in report?

Yes — your order confirmation email includes a note that your burn-in report will follow once your specific unit clears testing, so you can see the real numbers from the machine you're getting.

Warranty & returns

What does the warranty cover?

Every unit includes 3-year parts & labor cover as standard (upgradable to 5 years at checkout), covering manufacturing defects and component failure under normal use. It doesn't cover accidental damage or unauthorised modification.

Can I return a build if I change my mind?

Because each unit is configured to order, change-of-mind returns work differently than off-the-shelf products — see our Terms of Sale for the details. If your unit arrives faulty or not as described, that's a different situation and you're covered — contact support straight away.

How do I make a warranty claim?

Contact support with your order details and a description of the issue. Include your order confirmation email if you have it — it speeds things up.
