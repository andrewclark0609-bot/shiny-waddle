Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This is a template intended as a starting point, not legal advice. Data protection obligations (UK GDPR, EU GDPR, and equivalents elsewhere) depend on details specific to your business — have this reviewed by a solicitor before relying on it commercially, especially given you're processing orders internationally.

1. Who this applies to

This policy explains what personal data SIGNAL collects when you use this website, place an order, or contact support, and how that data is used.

2. What we collect

Depending on how you interact with the site, we may collect:

  • Order details: name, email address, phone number, and shipping address, collected during checkout.
  • Support enquiries: name, email address, and the content of any message you send through the support form.
  • Payment information: we never see or store your card details directly — these are entered directly into Stripe's own secure checkout.

3. Third parties we use

We rely on the following services to operate the site and process orders. Each has its own privacy policy governing how they handle data on our behalf:

4. Why we use your data

  • To process and fulfil your order, including shipping and warranty support
  • To respond to support enquiries
  • To meet our legal obligations, such as accounting and tax records

We do not sell your personal data, and we do not use it for advertising or marketing without your separate consent.

5. How long we keep it

We retain order records for as long as needed to meet accounting, tax, and warranty obligations (typically several years). Support enquiries are kept only as long as needed to resolve them, unless tied to an active order.

6. Your rights

If you're in the UK or EU, data protection law gives you the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Ask us to correct inaccurate data
  • Ask us to delete your data, where we're not required to keep it for legal reasons
  • Object to or restrict certain processing

To exercise any of these, contact us at andrewclark0609@gmail.com.

7. International orders

If you order from outside the UK, your data may be processed by our third-party providers in other countries as part of handling payment, shipping, and email delivery. Where required, we rely on those providers' own compliance mechanisms for international data transfers.

8. Cookies

This site does not currently use tracking or advertising cookies. Loading fonts from Google Fonts may involve a request to Google's servers, which is standard for most websites using web fonts.

9. Contact

Questions about this policy or how your data is handled: contact support or email andrewclark0609@gmail.com.
