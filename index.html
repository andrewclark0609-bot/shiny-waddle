What happens before it leaves the bench

  • 72-hour burn-in

    Mixed CPU/GPU stress and real game loops, logged continuously. Anything that drifts out of spec gets pulled and rebuilt.

  • Cable & airflow check

    Routing is inspected by hand for airflow clearance, not just looks — then photographed for your build record.

  • Report attached

    Your unit ships with its own burn-in log: clocks, temps, and frame-time data from the exact machine in the box.
